Our Courses

Introduction to Electronics

This course provides an overview of the fundamental principles of electronics. Topics include basic electronic components, circuit theory, and the operation of simple electronic devices. Students will gain hands-on experience through lab sessions and projects.

Communication Systems

This course covers the basics of communication systems, including analog and digital communication, modulation techniques, and data transmission. Students will learn about the various components and technologies used in modern communication systems.

Digital Signal Processing (DSP)

An in-depth study of digital signal processing, focusing on signal analysis, filtering, and transformation techniques. Students will work on practical applications and projects to understand the real-world implementation of DSP concepts.

Microprocessors and Microcontrollers

This course introduces students to the architecture and programming of microprocessors and microcontrollers. Topics include assembly language programming, interfacing techniques, and the design of embedded systems.
